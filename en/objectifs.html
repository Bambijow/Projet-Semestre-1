Objectives of a foreign intership

Discover / Learn a language

The principal objective at living in a foreign country is to adapt to the native language and to become bilingual. The learning of a language in a country is faster than the traditional learning.

Discover a new culture

The foreign internship allow to discover a new culture, discover new traditions and differents rituals than your native country. Spend your free time to visit and discover the city or even the country you are living in. This is useful to increase your knownledge.

Validation of your CV

The intership allow you to discover the professional world. Choose a foreign internship is better because you are learning from the studies, the work and you are leaning the language. This gives a lot more in your CV

The human experience

A foreign intership allows you to make a lot of acquintance especially during the studies and during work. This allow you to find more friends and to become open-minded.

How to write a CV in english

Writing a CV in English involves a lot of changes compared to a French CV, here are the main mistakes not to make:

-Check the true translation of words:

Some words seem transparent but in reality aren't said in the same way (For exemple "Work experience" resulting in "Éxpérience professionnelle").

-Don't confuse British and American CV, both have particularities. Committing a fault can be frowned upon by a business.

How to finance your stay abroad

To finance your stay, scholarships are put in place to help you like the students grants or the mobility aids
